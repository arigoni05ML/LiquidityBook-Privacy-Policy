Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE 11 JULY 2026

The Liquidity Book ("the app") is a futures trading journal published by Arigoni ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data you enter into the app

Everything you record in the app — trades, accounts, settings, notes, and trade screenshots — is stored only on your device. We do not operate any servers, we have no user accounts, and we never receive, see, or store your journal data. Backups you export (CSV or JSON files) are created locally and saved or shared only where you choose to send them.

Data deletion

You can delete all journal data at any time via Settings → Data → Delete all data, or by uninstalling the app. Because we hold no copy of your data, deletion on your device is complete and permanent.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process information such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, coarse location, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. This collection is performed by Google, not by us. See how Google uses data from apps that use its services and the Google Privacy Policy.

Where required (for example in the EEA and UK), you will be shown a consent form before personalized ads are served. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time in the app via Settings → App → Manage ad consent. You can also reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization in your device's Google settings.

Permissions

  • Notifications — used only if you enable the optional daily reminder to log your trades. You can disable it in the app or in system settings at any time.
  • Biometrics — used only if you enable the optional app lock. Biometric verification happens entirely on your device via Android; the app never accesses your fingerprint or face data.
  • Photos / media — used only when you choose to attach chart screenshots to a trade or save a P&L recap image to your gallery. The app never scans or uploads your photo library.
  • Storage — on older Android versions, used only when you choose to save a backup file to your device's Documents folder.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
