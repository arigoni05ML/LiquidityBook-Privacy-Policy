Your journal is stored on this device (and mirrored to durable app storage in the mobile app). Back up regularly anyway — a backup file is the only thing that survives losing the device. "Save backup to Files" writes to Documents/The Liquidity Book; "Backup — share" opens the share sheet (Drive, email, …). Trade screenshots live in app storage and are not included in the JSON backup.
